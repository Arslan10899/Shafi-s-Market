About the Founder

Muhammad Arslan Munir is the visionary founder and CEO of AZ Mart, an innovative affiliate marketplace that connects users with the best deals from top global platforms including Amazon, Alibaba, AliExpress, and Daraz.

With a passion for e-commerce and digital innovation, Arslan founded AZ Mart with the mission to create a platform where anyone can discover, compare, and share products from multiple global merchants — all in one place. His vision empowers users to become affiliate marketers themselves, earning through product sharing while helping others find the best deals.

Under his leadership, AZ Mart has grown into a comprehensive affiliate ecosystem that supports both buyers and sellers, featuring product management, affiliate link tracking, and a user-friendly dashboard experience.

Arslan is committed to continuous improvement and innovation, always looking for new ways to enhance the platform and provide more value to the AZ Mart community.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the leading affiliate marketplace in the region, empowering individuals to earn through product sharing while providing shoppers access to the best deals worldwide.

Mission

To democratize affiliate marketing by providing a simple, powerful platform where anyone can add products, share affiliate links, and earn commissions — regardless of technical expertise.
